Motorcycles, driven by gasoline engines, are two or three-wheeled vehicles with front wheel steering by hand. They are portable, flexible and fast. In general, motorcycles are divided into streetcars, road racing motorcycles, cross-country motorcycles, cruise cars, wagons and so on.
